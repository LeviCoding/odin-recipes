Pancakes

Description

Yammi pancakes, made in a wink

Ingredients for about 6 pancakes

  • 250g flower
  • 250ml milk
  • 2x eggs
  • 50ml sparkling water
  • bit of suggar and salt

Steps

  1. mix folwer and milk
  2. add the eggs
  3. add sparkling water
  4. finally add bit of suggar and salt
  5. let it rest for 10 mins
  6. Ready for action!
